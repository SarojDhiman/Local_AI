Artificial Intelligence Syllabus:
1. Introduction to AI: History and major applications.
2. Machine Learning: Supervised learning, unsupervised learning, and reinforcement learning.
3. Neural Networks and Deep Learning: Perceptrons, convolutional neural networks (CNNs), and recurrent neural networks (RNNs).
4. Natural Language Processing (NLP): Language models, text classification, and sentiment analysis.
5. Computer Vision: Object detection, image recognition, and image segmentation.
6. Ethics in AI: Bias, fairness, and responsible AI development.
